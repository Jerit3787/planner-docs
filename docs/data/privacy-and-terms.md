# Privacy & terms

Student Planner's full Privacy Policy and Terms and Conditions are hosted on the app itself, so they
always reflect exactly what's currently deployed:

- [Privacy Policy](https://planner.danplace.tech/privacy.html) — what data the app collects, why, and
  what control you have over it.
- [Terms and Conditions](https://planner.danplace.tech/terms.html) — the terms of using the app.
- [Open Source Licenses](https://planner.danplace.tech/licenses.html) — the third-party libraries the
  app is built on and their licenses.

## The short version

- Guest data never leaves your device.
- An account's data is stored in a private, access-controlled database — only you can read or write
  your own records.
- Your data is never sold or shared with advertisers.
- You can export everything or permanently delete your account at any time from
  [Settings](../features/settings.md).

For the full details, read the linked pages above — they're kept short and in plain language on
purpose.
